<h2><span id="What_Are_the_NBA_Teams_and_Their_Colors">What Are the NBA Teams and Their Colors?</span></h2>



<p>The NBA is a professional basketball league with 30 teams (29 teams from the United States and 1 team from Canada). NBA stands for National Basketball Association. NBA Team Colors in RGB, Pantone, HEX, and CMYK can be found in the respective tables below.</p>



<p>The NBA has 2 conferences – western and eastern. 15 NBA teams play in the western conference and 15 NBA teams play in the eastern conference.</p>
